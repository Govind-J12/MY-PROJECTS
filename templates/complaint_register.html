Top features of our complaint box system

Anonymity: Many systems allow for anonymous submissions, which can encourage more people to voice concerns without fear of retaliation or embarrassment.

Efficiency: Complaints can be logged, tracked, and managed more efficiently. Automated systems can categorize and prioritize issues, streamlining the resolution process.

Transparency: An online system can provide visibility into the status of complaints, including acknowledgments, updates, and resolutions, which fosters transparency and trust in the process.

Actions we will take regarding your complaints

Acknowledge Receipt: Faculty will usually send an acknowledgment message to confirm they have received the complaint. This helps reassure the student that their issue is being taken seriously.

Review the Complaint: Faculty will carefully review the details of the complaint. This may involve checking relevant documents, assignments, and communications to understand the issue fully..

Assess the Situation: Faculty will evaluate the complaint against institutional policies, course guidelines, and any relevant precedents. They may also consider any extenuating circumstances that might have contributed to the issue.

Implement Solutions: Based on the findings, faculty will implement appropriate solutions or corrective actions. This could involve adjusting grades, providing additional support, or modifying course policies.
